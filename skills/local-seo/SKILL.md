# Local SEO: Differentiation Strategy

Analyze local pages through the lens of **"H1 for Google, H2 for Humans"**.

## Core Principle

Every local entity says the same generic things. Churches say "welcoming community." Roofers say "trusted since 1987." Lawyers say "fighting for you."

**Nobody cares.** Your competitor says the same thing.

What converts: **Differentiation.** Give people a reason to pick you over the 15 other options Google shows them.

## Analysis Framework

When analyzing a page, examine these layers:

### 1. SEO Layer (For Google)

- **H1**: Does it contain [Entity Type] + [Location]?
- **Title tag**: Is it keyword-optimized?
- **Meta description**: Does it compel clicks from SERP?
- **Schema markup**: Is structured data present and complete?
- **Internal linking**: Are related pages connected?

### 2. Conversion Layer (For Humans)

- **H2/Subheadline**: Does it differentiate or just describe?
- **Value proposition**: Why choose this over alternatives?
- **Social proof**: Reviews, testimonials, credentials visible above fold?
- **CTA clarity**: Is the next action obvious and compelling?

### 3. Content Quality

- **Specificity**: Concrete details vs. vague claims
- **Proof points**: Evidence for claims made
- **User intent match**: Does content answer what searchers want?

## Differentiation by Entity Type

### Commercial Services (Roofing, HVAC, Plumbing, etc.)

Differentiate through **offers with specificity**:

- Specific dollar amounts: "$500 off" not "big savings"
- Risk reversal: "Beat any quote by 10% or inspection free"
- Urgency: "24-hour emergency, no extra charge"
- Value stacking: "Free diagnostic + first 3 months financing free"

### Churches & Religious Organizations

Differentiate through **unique identity and invitation**:

- Worship style: "Contemporary worship with a 12-piece band" vs. "Traditional liturgy with pipe organ"
- Community specifics: "200+ young professionals" or "Multi-generational with active seniors ministry"
- Practical value: "Childcare during all services" / "Spanish interpretation available"
- Programs: "Weekly community dinner" / "Recovery groups meeting Tuesdays"
- Accessibility: "ASL interpretation" / "Wheelchair accessible sanctuary"

**Avoid**: "Welcoming community" / "Come as you are" / "A place to belong" (everyone says this)

### Professional Services (Legal, Medical, Financial)

Differentiate through **expertise and process clarity**:

- Specialization: "Exclusively personal injury for 20 years"
- Process: "Free case evaluation within 24 hours"
- Results: "97% success rate on contested cases"
- Risk: "No fee unless we win"

### Restaurants & Hospitality

Differentiate through **experience and proof**:

- Specifics: "Wood-fired pizza, dough made fresh daily"
- Recognition: "#1 rated on Yelp 3 years running"
- Unique: "Only restaurant in [city] with [feature]"

## Analysis Process

When given a file or URL:

1. **Read the content** - Identify H1, H2, meta tags, key messaging
2. **Identify entity type** - Commercial, religious, professional, etc.
3. **Flag generic messaging** - What do competitors also say?
4. **Find differentiation opportunities** - What's unique that isn't highlighted?
5. **Assess conversion path** - Is the CTA clear and compelling?
6. **Check technical SEO** - Schema, titles, structure

---

## Red Flags (Generic Messaging)

| Phrase                           | Problem                             |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| "Top rated" / "Best in [city]"   | Unsubstantiated, everyone claims it |
| "Trusted since [year]"           | Irrelevant unless you explain why   |
| "Family owned and operated"      | Doesn't answer "why should I care?" |
| "Welcoming community"            | Every church says this              |
| "Quality workmanship"            | Prove it, don't claim it            |
| "Full service" / "Comprehensive" | Vague, says nothing specific        |
| "Serving the greater [area]"     | Geography isn't differentiation     |
| "Licensed and insured"           | Table stakes, not a selling point   |
| "Free estimates"                 | Everyone offers this                |

## Green Flags (Differentiation)

| Element          | Why It Works                                                 |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Specific numbers | "$500 off" / "200+ members" / "3 services weekly"            |
| Named programs   | "Alpha course" / "Celebrate Recovery" / "Young Adults group" |
| Time specifics   | "24-hour response" / "Same-day appointments"                 |
| Risk reversal    | "Or it's free" / "Money-back guarantee"                      |
| Unique features  | Only one in area with X                                      |
| Proof points     | Awards, ratings, certifications, testimonials                |
| Process clarity  | "Here's exactly what happens when you visit"                 |

---

## Output Approach

Provide analysis as prose, not rigid templates. Cover:

1. **Current State Summary** - What's working, what's generic
2. **SEO Assessment** - H1, titles, schema, keyword optimization
3. **Conversion Assessment** - Value prop, CTA, differentiation
4. **Specific Recommendations** - Prioritized list of improvements
5. **Implementation** - If asked, provide code/content changes

Be specific to the entity type. A church needs different optimization than a plumber.

---

## Guidelines

- **Analyze first** - Understand what exists before recommending
- **Be entity-aware** - Different strategies for different types
- **Prioritize impact** - Focus on high-leverage changes
- **Stay practical** - Recommendations should be implementable
- **Preserve voice** - Improvements shouldn't sound like a different entity
